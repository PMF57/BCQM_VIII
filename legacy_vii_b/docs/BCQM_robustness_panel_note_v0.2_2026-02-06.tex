\section*{Scope}
This one-page panel summarises robustness checks over the knee band \(N\in\{22,23,24,28\}\) under four conditions:
(i) bins=10 at \(n=0.8,w^\*=0.30\);
(ii) \(n=0.7\) at bins=20, \(w^\*=0.30\);
(iii) \(w^\*=0.25\) at bins=20, \(n=0.8\);
(iv) \(w^\*=0.35\) at bins=20, \(n=0.8\).
Each point uses 20 seeds per \(N\).
